## 📌 Overview
This project presents an **Account Statement Insights Dashboard** developed using **Microsoft Excel** to analyze personal or organizational financial transactions between **July and December**.

The dashboard provides a clear breakdown of income, expenses, transaction volume, spending categories, and monthly balances. It is designed to help users track financial behavior and make better financial decisions.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement
Managing financial records manually can make it difficult to understand spending patterns and maintain financial control.

Common challenges include:
- Difficulty tracking total income and expenses  
- Lack of visibility into spending categories  
- Poor monitoring of account balance over time  
- Inability to identify major expense areas  
- Limited understanding of transaction frequency  

This project solves these problems by converting raw account data into an interactive and easy-to-understand dashboard.

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- **Microsoft Excel**
  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning and Preparation
The dataset was prepared to ensure accuracy and usability:

- Removed duplicate transactions  
- Handled missing or inconsistent entries  
- Standardized transaction categories (e.g., bills, airtime, POS, transfers)  
- Formatted currency values properly  
- Organized data into structured tables  

---

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
EDA was carried out to uncover financial patterns:

- Calculated total credit, debit, and transaction count  
- Analyzed expenses by category  
- Examined monthly income vs expenses (cashflow)  
- Tracked balance trends over time  
- Identified top expense categories  

---

## 📈 Results and Findings
Key insights from the dashboard include:

- Total credit significantly exceeds total debit, indicating positive cash flow  
- Bills and transfers account for the largest portion of expenses  
- Monthly income remains relatively stable, while expenses fluctuate  
- Account balance dropped sharply at one point before recovering  
- A few expense categories dominate overall spending  

---

## 💡 Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are suggested:

- Monitor high-spending categories such as bills and transfers  
- Set monthly spending limits to control expenses  
- Maintain consistent savings from surplus income  
- Track balance changes regularly to avoid sudden drops  
- Reduce unnecessary or repetitive expenses
